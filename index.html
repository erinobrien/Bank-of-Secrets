What is Bank of Secrets?

Bank of Secrets is a place to post your deepest and darkest secrets and in return, earn money! Yup, it's that simple. Each secret you post will have a monetary value placed on it (but only after you hit post!) and your earnings are kept in your Bank of Secrets wallet to deposit into your verified bank account at your own leisure. Unfortunately due to the nature of the site, Bank of Secrets is only available for those aged 18 years and older.

How it works

By simply signing up with approval from your bank, you can create your own public profile, follow your friends and post as many secrets as you want! As well as your followers, anyone on the site can view your posts and like or dislike them. This is where the risk begins! Before posting your secret, you will place a value for your secret to increase (when someone likes) and decrease (when someone dislikes) by. The more scandelous the secret, the more money you may earn!
